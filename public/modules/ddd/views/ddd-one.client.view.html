Order CARFAX Report

When purchasing a car, it is vital to know it's history so that you know exactly what you are getting. One of the best ways to do this is with a CARFAX report. CARFAX Reports contain important information that can help you make a more informed decision about a used car, truck or SUV, including:

  • Vehicle Registration
  • Frame/Structural Damage
  • Salvaged, Junk, or Flood titles
  • Odometer Readings
  • Lemon History
  • Total Loss Accident History
  • Accident Indicators
  • Service and Repair Information
  • Vehicle Usage (was it ever a taxi or rental car)
  • Recall Information

You can easily order a CARFAX report online. Some types of CARFAX reports are free. However, there is a fee to order most detailed reports, and you will need one of the following to do this:

  • Vehicle Identification Number (VIN)
  • License Plate Number and State the vehicle is registered in.

If you do not have either of these, you may need to contact the seller to obtain them to order the report.
